Tyrowo's Procrastination Buster

In an attempt to limit my intake of Reddit and other habit-use websites, I created a Google Chrome extension.
It's a sort of voluntary parental advisory software, where you input the websites you have a problem with visiting too frequently, how much time you want to be allowed on those sites, and how long you want to be kicked off for.
The extension then allows a visit for the length of time specified before kicking you out of all the tabs you have open of it, and then uses declarative net requests to prohibit access to the website.

It was written with Chrome's most recent Manifest V3 extension protocols, and carries your preferences across devices.
The link above is to my extension's verified page on the Chrome Web Store.
